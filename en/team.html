Faculty

Yuncheol Kang (강윤철)

Email: yckang at ewha dot ac dot kr

Since March 2020, Associate Professor at the College of Business, Ewha Womans University. Previously served at the Department of Industrial Engineering (now the Department of Industrial Data Engineering), Hongik University (2016–2020). B.S. in Industrial Engineering from KAIST; M.S. from Seoul National University (Advisor: Jinwoo Park); Ph.D. from The Pennsylvania State University (Advisor: Vittaldas Prabhu). Prior industry experience includes systems engineering at LG CNS and contributions to electronic passport system initiatives in Korea. Experience also includes leadership in IT system development projects associated with public services in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania, USA.

Research interests center on the integration of information technology and decision-making methodologies. Current work focuses on artificial intelligence approaches to sequential decision optimization, with applications in manufacturing, logistics, healthcare, and the public sector.

- Sequential decision-making and simulation-based optimization (e.g., Reinforcement Learning)
- Human–AI interaction (e.g., explainability, Human-in-the-Loop systems)
- Data analytics integrated with optimization (e.g., prediction, diagnosis, classification)

Courses: Sequential Decision Making, Business Statistics, Business Data Analytics

Research Collaborators

Seongsoo (Simon) Jang (장성수)

Email: JangS at Cardiff dot ac dot uk

Professor Seongsoo Jang joined the AAPL Research Group as a Research Collaborator in December 2023. Currently serves as Reader of Empirical Marketing (Associate Professor of Marketing) at Cardiff Business School, Cardiff University, United Kingdom. Brings approximately 15 years of industry experience from SK Telecom and SK Planet, with professional expertise spanning international roaming, marketing and product strategy, mobile app markets, and business intelligence. Research focuses on empirical marketing based on real-world quantitative data. Specific areas of interest include Digital Marketing, Retail Marketing, CSR/ESG Management, Health & Social Care Analytics, and Data-driven Better Marketing for a Better Society. Author of more than 30 SSCI-indexed journal articles. Actively engaged in industry–academia collaboration and consulting projects with UK and Korean firms to support data-driven decision-making. Delivers executive lectures on “Quantitative Intuition-Based Decision Making” for corporate audiences (e.g., SK University, SK Broadband) and MBA managers. Also serves as a visiting professor at several international institutions, including the University of Surrey, Xiamen University, and the Institute of Management and Technology Dubai.
